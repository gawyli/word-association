Welcome to the Word Association Game

This game helps you explore word associations and improve your vocabulary. Here's how to play:

  1. Click on "Start Session" in the menu to begin a new game.
  2. You'll be presented with a stimulus word.
  3. Respond with the first word that comes to mind.
  4. Continue this process until you're ready to end the session.
  5. View your progress and word associations in the "Graph" section.

Previous sessions are listed on the left side of the screen. Click on a session to view its details.
